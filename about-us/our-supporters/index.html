Our Supporters

Many thanks to the following organisations who have given us fantastic support.

 

Social Entrepreneurs Ireland aims to ignite social change by identifying, investing in, and supporting Irish social entrepreneurs – individuals who are prepared to take risks by applying their energy, drive, ambition and innovation to tackle some of Ireland’s most entrenched social problems.  Both co-founders of MissionV, James Corbett and Margaret Keane, are Social Entrepreneurs Ireland Awardees.

Edgecast Media Ltd is a multi-award winning  radio and digital media production company based in County Clare.  They produce audio and video, for broadcast and for online distribution and offer turn key media solutions to their clients.

Clare Training Resource Centre, a division of  the Clare Family Resource Centre is located in Ennis, Co. Clare. It is a high quality venue offering the community affordable meeting rooms and training facilities that can accommodate a variety of activities for both small and large groups.
